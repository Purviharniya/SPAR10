Our text classification tool is a multialgo-ML classification engine customised for providing two main use cases namely, Document classification and even Product Classification. Load your labelled training data, our engine learns using the ensemble ML models and chooses the best model to fit the best-suited label. SPAR10 will help you in configuring the initial setup and retune the models.

Tech Stack

The various Python libraries used for classification of structured and unstructured data.

Text Classification

Library Used: Tensorflow, Keras, SpaCy, scikit-learn, NumPy, Pandas, NLTK, H5py

Document Classification

Library Used: Tensorflow, Keras scikit-learn, NumPy, Pandas, Pickle, Matplotlib, H5py

In a Nutshell

All you need to do with our text categorization software is to assign a category to each document (letter, form, email, handwritten, advertisement, scientific report, scientific publication, specification, file folder, news article budget, invoice, presentation, questionnaire, resume, memo). It is also capable of working as a text categorization tool to categorize the data into sub-categories.

Functions (Use Cases)

  • Product categorization/classification for an E-commerce business
  • Identify & recognize the different types of documents and automatically despatch to respective departments for any business.
  • Automatic extraction, validation and redirection to respective departments of business emails.
  • Redaction of sensitive information extracted from documents such as Bank statements, EHRs, Invoices, KYC, Emails, Legal documents, Research papers, and more.
Ready-made pre-processing and training pipeline for any kind of labelled text data.
Multiple categories supported for classification.
